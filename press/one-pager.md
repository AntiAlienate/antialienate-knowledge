# AntiAlienate.com — One-Page Fact Sheet for Journalists

## What it is
The largest open-source knowledge base on parental alienation. Free. CC BY 4.0. No paywall.

## Where to find it
[github.com/AntiAlienate/antialienate-knowledge](https://github.com/AntiAlienate/antialienate-knowledge)

## What makes it newsworthy

1. **First of its kind**: no comparable open-source PA reference exists at this scale.
2. **Auto-accumulating**: a background agent polls 5 primary sources every 25 minutes and adds fresh material daily.
3. **Cross-jurisdictional**: spans US, UK, EU, Australia, Canada.
4. **Free in a paywalled field**: most equivalent material costs $200+/month via legal databases or is locked behind academic publishers.
5. **Names credited transparently**: profiles the researchers whose work targeted parents need to find.

## Quotable angles

- **Public-interest tech**: AI-assisted curation applied to an under-served social issue.
- **Family-court reform**: data infrastructure to support the growing legal recognition of PA.
- **Open-source models for civic knowledge**: replicable approach for other under-documented family-law domains.
