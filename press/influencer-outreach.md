# Influencer & Expert Outreach List — DRAFT

The named clinicians and researchers in `/influencers/` should hear about this launch from us, ideally before the press release goes out. Their endorsement, retweet, or even a "I'll take a look" is exponentially more valuable than paid distribution.

## How to use this list

1. **Personalise every email.** No mass send. Each contact below has a specific reason we're approaching them.
2. **Lead with their work.** First paragraph references something specific they've published.
3. **Make the ask small.** "We'd love your eyes on it; if you find it useful, we'd be honoured if you'd share." Not "please retweet."
4. **No favours expected.** Open-source ethos. They get the resource regardless of response.
5. **Track in a simple sheet** — name, date sent, response (none / opened / replied / shared).

With gratitude for your work,

Alan Markson
AntiAlienate.com

## What to do after sending

- **Don't follow up within two weeks.** Researchers are busy. A single follow-up at the 3-week mark is fine.
- **If they reply with corrections**, ship them within 24 hours.
- **If they share publicly**, thank them publicly and amplify their original post.
- **If silent**, don't take it personally. The resource exists regardless.
