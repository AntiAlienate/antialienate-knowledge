# Global Parental-Alienation Jurisdictional Index

## TL;DR

This is the single navigation hub for AntiAlienate's comprehensive case-law repository. As of May 2026, the repo covers **47 jurisdictions** across 8 regional blocks plus a substantial research and clinical reference layer. All entries follow a standard schema: statutory framework, Supreme Court / Constitutional Court jurisprudence, ECHR (where applicable), practical motion language, cross-border considerations, citing antialienate.com posts, and authoritative sources.
