Mission Brief

Target: 100,000 visitors / day to antialienate.com.

Vectors:

  1. BACKFILL — migrate 65 vault posts → repo-formatted markdown with TL;DR, H2/H3 sections, citations, source-blog hyperlinks, Alan Markson byline, CC BY 4.0 footer.
  2. WIKI ENRICHMENT — 3–4 new wiki entries per loop tick into /case-law/, /research/, /clinical/, /statutes/ with YAML frontmatter and citing-posts tables linking back to blog URLs.
  3. ALAN VIDEOS — HeyGen vertical scripts; paused per directive.
  4. NEW CASE LAW — actively scan 2024–2026 ECHR, Belgian Cour de cassation, French, German BGH, Spanish, AU Federal Circuit & Family Court.
  5. STATUS — update this dashboard, queue, fleet publish event with everything pushed.

Hard rules: never POST/PUT/DELETE against antialienate.com. Always Alan Markson byline. Use $(cat /tmp/.aa-secrets/...) — never echo literals.

Stop criteria: only when Adam says stop, or cron auto-expires after 7 days.
