# Poland — Parental Alienation Legal Reference

> Jurisdiction-specific legal reference for parents, lawyers, and clinicians navigating parental-alienation matters in Poland. Cross-referenced with deeply-investigated case studies. CC BY 4.0.

## Quick reference

| Element | Poland position |
|---|---|
| Statutory PA recognition | No syndromic recognition. *Kodeks rodzinny i opiekuńczy* (KRO) art. 113 guarantees the child's right to contact with each parent; the *Kodeks postępowania cywilnego* (k.p.c.) arts. 598¹⁵–598²² provide a two-stage financial-penalty enforcement regime against custodial parents who obstruct contact ("alienacja rodzicielska"). |
| Apex court direction (current) | Counter-trend re-opening enforcement. [Uchwała Sądu Najwyższego z 3 października 2025 r., III CZP 20/25](../case-studies/stj-iii-czp-20-25-uchwala-sad-najwyzszy-alienacja-rodzicielska-2025.md) restored the enforcement regime against custodial parents whose own conduct produced the child's "refusal" of contact, after the 2022 Constitutional Tribunal ruling SK 3/20 had narrowed it. |
| Standard of proof for PA findings | Civil standard (*swobodna ocena dowodów*) under art. 233 § 1 k.p.c.; *dobro dziecka* governs. |
| Forensic-evaluator framework | *Opinia biegłego* under k.p.c. arts. 278 ff.; **OZSS** (Opiniodawcze Zespoły Sądowych Specjalistów) — court-appointed multidisciplinary teams. |
| Reunification programs court-ordered | *Kurator sądowy* supervision; *rodzinne ośrodki* therapeutic interventions; *terapia rodzinna* on court order; no coercive residential model. |
| Domestic-abuse counterweight in place | Yes. *Ustawa o przeciwdziałaniu przemocy domowej* (2005, substantially amended 2023); *Niebieska Karta* protocol; Istanbul Convention ratified (with reservations) 2015. |

## Statutory framework

- **Konstytucja RP, art. 48 i art. 72** — parental rights and rights of the child. [Sejm — Konstytucja](https://www.sejm.gov.pl/prawo/konst/polski/kon1.htm).
- **Kodeks rodzinny i opiekuńczy (KRO), 1964** — substantive family law. Art. 113 (right of contact); arts. 109 ff. (ograniczenie / pozbawienie władzy rodzicielskiej). [ISAP — KRO](https://isap.sejm.gov.pl/isap.nsf/DocDetails.xsp?id=WDU19640090059).
- **Kodeks postępowania cywilnego (k.p.c.)**:
  - Arts. 598¹⁵–598²² — two-stage enforcement of contact orders (threat then imposition of financial penalty). [ISAP — k.p.c.](https://isap.sejm.gov.pl/isap.nsf/DocDetails.xsp?id=WDU19640430296).
  - Arts. 278–291 — *opinia biegłego* (expert opinion).
- **Ustawa o opiniodawczych zespołach sądowych specjalistów (OZSS), 2015** — establishment of court-appointed multidisciplinary teams replacing the older *Rodzinne Ośrodki Diagnostyczno-Konsultacyjne* (RODK). [ISAP](https://isap.sejm.gov.pl/isap.nsf/DocDetails.xsp?id=WDU20150000509).
- **Ustawa o przeciwdziałaniu przemocy domowej, 2005** (amended 2023). [ISAP](https://isap.sejm.gov.pl/isap.nsf/DocDetails.xsp?id=WDU20051801493).
- **Konwencja Stambulska** — Council of Europe Convention on preventing and combating violence against women and domestic violence (Istanbul); ratified by Poland 2015.

## Apex court direction

The leading recent ruling is **[Uchwała Sądu Najwyższego z 3 października 2025 r., III CZP 20/25](../case-studies/stj-iii-czp-20-25-uchwala-sad-najwyzszy-alienacja-rodzicielska-2025.md)**. The Sąd Najwyższy (Izba Cywilna) resolved a legal question referred under art. 390 § 1 k.p.c. arising from divergent regional-court practice after the **Trybunał Konstytucyjny wyrok z 22 czerwca 2022 r., SK 3/20** ([Trybunał Konstytucyjny case file](https://trybunal.gov.pl/postepowanie-i-orzeczenia/wyroki/art/12025-wykonywanie-orzeczen-w-przedmiocie-kontaktow-z-dzieckiem)).

- **SK 3/20 (2022)** had held that art. 598¹⁶ § 1 k.p.c. was incompatible with the constitutional protection of family life *insofar as* it permitted a financial penalty against a custodial parent where contact failure was the consequence of the child's own attitude (*postawa dziecka*).
- That ruling produced jurisprudential conflict: some courts read SK 3/20 as a blanket immunity for any custodial parent who could show the child "refused"; others continued to enforce on the view that the child's refusal was itself the product of the custodial parent's alienating conduct.
- **III CZP 20/25** resolved the conflict by holding that the SK 3/20 immunity does not apply where the custodial parent's own conduct has produced the child's refusal. In effect, the SN re-opened the enforcement regime for cases of demonstrated *alienacja rodzicielska*.

The decision is a counter-trend among European apex courts that have generally moved away from PA-friendly enforcement.

The **Trybunał Konstytucyjny** is the constitutional-review apex; the **Sąd Najwyższy** is the cassation court for civil and criminal matters.

## Standard of proof

Polish civil procedure operates under *swobodna ocena dowodów* per art. 233 § 1 k.p.c. — the court evaluates evidence freely but according to *zasady doświadczenia życiowego i logiki*. Family-court orders affecting *władza rodzicielska* or contact are governed by *dobro dziecka* (KRO art. 95 and CRC art. 3).

## Forensic evaluation framework

- **Opinia biegłego** under k.p.c. arts. 278 ff. — court-appointed expert.
- **OZSS (Opiniodawcze Zespoły Sądowych Specjalistów)** — multidisciplinary teams (typically psycholog + pedagog) attached to *sądy okręgowe*; replaced the older RODK regime in 2016. Provide opinions in family-court matters involving children.
- **Kurator sądowy** — court-appointed probation/family officer, principal monitoring resource for compliance with parenting orders.
- **Rzecznik Praw Dziecka** — Ombudsman for Children; has standing to intervene in proceedings.

## Reunification programs

- **Kurator sądowy supervision** — supervised contact and monitoring of compliance with contact orders.
- **Therapeutic interventions** through *poradnie psychologiczno-pedagogiczne* and *rodzinne ośrodki*.
- **Mediacja rodzinna** under k.p.c. — court-encouraged family mediation.
- The two-stage financial-penalty mechanism under arts. 598¹⁵–598²² k.p.c. is the principal *coercive* tool; it functions as an indirect reunification mechanism by raising the cost of obstructive conduct.
- US/Anglo-style coercive residential reunification camps are not part of the Polish toolkit.

## Domestic-violence interaction

- **Ustawa o przeciwdziałaniu przemocy domowej (2005, 2023)** — DV framework; *zakaz zbliżania się* and *nakaz opuszczenia mieszkania* available.
- **Niebieska Karta** — interagency DV protocol involving police, social services, education, and health.
- **Konwencja Stambulska** — Poland's ratification has been politically contested; GREVIO follow-up reports have flagged family-court treatment of DV.
- The SN's III CZP 20/25 ruling acknowledges that the custodial-parent enforcement regime cannot be used in cases where DV findings independently justify contact restrictions; the doctrine distinguishes between child refusal grounded in DV and refusal grounded in alienating conduct.

## Honest gaps

- Panel composition (skład orzekający) of III CZP 20/25 is **not publicly confirmed** in the public commentary surveyed and requires verification from the SN docket.
- The status of Polish ratification of the Istanbul Convention is politically volatile; this reference does not track current status.
- The 2023 amendments to the *Ustawa o przeciwdziałaniu przemocy domowej* have produced practice-level shifts (especially *nakaz natychmiastowego opuszczenia*) that are not yet fully captured in published jurisprudence.
