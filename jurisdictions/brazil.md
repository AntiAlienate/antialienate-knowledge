# Brazil — Parental Alienation Legal Reference

> Jurisdiction-specific legal reference for parents, lawyers, and clinicians navigating parental-alienation matters in Brazil. Cross-referenced with deeply-investigated case studies. CC BY 4.0.

## Quick reference

| Element | Brazil position |
|---|---|
| Statutory PA recognition | Yes — the most explicit statutory regime among major legal systems. **Lei nº 12.318, de 26 de agosto de 2010** defines *alienação parental* and prescribes a graduated catalogue of judicial remedies. The statute is currently under live revocation pressure (PL 1.372/2023). |
| Apex court direction (current) | "No-automaticity" doctrine. STJ Terceira Turma — see [REsp 1.859.228/SP (Rel. Min. Nancy Andrighi)](../case-studies/stj-resp-1859228-sp-nancy-andrighi-alienacao-parental-2024.md) and [REsp 2.108.750/GO](../case-studies/stj-resp-2108750-go-alienacao-parental-abuso-sexual-2024.md) — has held that inversion of custody under art. 6º, V is not automatic on a PA finding, requires *perícia* under art. 5º, and may not be used to override sexual-abuse allegations without adequate evidentiary scrutiny. |
| Standard of proof for PA findings | Civil standard (*livre convencimento motivado*) under art. 371 CPC/2015; *melhor interesse da criança* under CRC and ECA. |
| Forensic-evaluator framework | *Perícia psicológica ou biopsicossocial* under art. 5º of Lei 12.318/2010; *equipe interprofissional* of the *Vara da Família*; *escuta especializada* per ECA / Lei 13.431/2017. |
| Reunification programs court-ordered | Graduated remedies under art. 6º of Lei 12.318/2010 (advertência → ampliação do regime de convivência → multa → alteração da guarda → suspensão da autoridade parental). |
| Domestic-abuse counterweight in place | Yes — *Lei Maria da Penha* (Lei 11.340/2006); *Lei 13.431/2017* on *escuta especializada*; CNJ Protocolo 2024 on child-hearing in PA cases. |

## Statutory framework

- **Lei nº 12.318, de 26 de agosto de 2010** — *dispõe sobre a alienação parental*. Art. 2º defines *alienação parental*; art. 5º mandates *perícia psicológica ou biopsicossocial*; art. 6º lists graduated remedies. [Planalto](https://www.planalto.gov.br/ccivil_03/_ato2007-2010/2010/lei/l12318.htm).
- **Código Civil, arts. 1.583–1.589** — *guarda* (custody) regime, including *guarda compartilhada* as legal default under Lei 13.058/2014. [Planalto](https://www.planalto.gov.br/ccivil_03/leis/2002/l10406compilada.htm).
- **Lei nº 8.069, de 13 de julho de 1990 (Estatuto da Criança e do Adolescente — ECA)** — child-welfare framework, intervention of *Ministério Público*, *Conselho Tutelar*. [Planalto](https://www.planalto.gov.br/ccivil_03/leis/l8069.htm).
- **Lei nº 13.431, de 4 de abril de 2017** — *escuta especializada* and *depoimento especial* of children and adolescents. [Planalto](https://www.planalto.gov.br/ccivil_03/_ato2015-2018/2017/lei/l13431.htm).
- **Lei nº 11.340, de 7 de agosto de 2006 (Lei Maria da Penha)** — DV-specific protective regime. [Planalto](https://www.planalto.gov.br/ccivil_03/_ato2004-2006/2006/lei/l11340.htm).
- **Constituição Federal de 1988, art. 227** — *prioridade absoluta* of children's rights. [Planalto](https://www.planalto.gov.br/ccivil_03/constituicao/constituicao.htm).
- **Live revocation bill — Projeto de Lei nº 1.372/2023** — proposes integral revocation of Lei 12.318/2010. Approved by the *Comissão de Constituição e Justiça* of the Câmara dos Deputados on 3 December 2025 (37–28). [Câmara dos Deputados — PL 1.372/2023](https://www.camara.leg.br/proposicoesWeb/fichadetramitacao?idProposicao=2354956).

## Apex court direction

The STJ Terceira Turma has built a doctrine sometimes characterised as "no-automaticity":

- **[REsp 1.859.228/SP (Rel. Min. Nancy Andrighi)](../case-studies/stj-resp-1859228-sp-nancy-andrighi-alienacao-parental-2024.md)** — inversion of custody under art. 6º, V of Lei 12.318/2010 is not the default response to an alienating-conduct finding; the family court must apply the graduated catalogue starting with less invasive remedies and must consider the child's emotional stability in the current placement.

- **[REsp 2.108.750/GO](../case-studies/stj-resp-2108750-go-alienacao-parental-abuso-sexual-2024.md)** — where the case file contains allegations of sexual abuse against the parent invoking alienation, the family court may not collapse the abuse evaluation into the PA framework; *perícia* under art. 5º must independently engage with the abuse allegations.

- The STF (Supremo Tribunal Federal) has not issued a definitive constitutional ruling on Lei 12.318/2010; PL 1.372/2023 may produce STF litigation regardless of legislative outcome.

The 2024 **Protocolo para escuta de menor em ações de alienação parental** approved by the CNJ provides procedural guidance on child-hearing in PA matters. [STJ news, 19 September 2024](https://www.stj.jus.br/sites/portalp/Paginas/Comunicacao/Noticias/2024/19092024-CNJ-aprova-propostas-de-grupo-de-trabalho-para-depoimento-de-criancas-e-adolescentes-sobre-alienacao-parental.aspx).

## Standard of proof

Brazilian civil procedure operates under *livre convencimento motivado* (CPC/2015, art. 371). Family-court findings affecting *guarda*, *convivência* or *autoridade parental* must be reasoned in light of *prioridade absoluta* (CF/88 art. 227) and *melhor interesse da criança* (CRC art. 3, internalised via Decreto 99.710/1990).

## Forensic evaluation framework

- **Perícia psicológica ou biopsicossocial** under art. 5º of Lei 12.318/2010 — mandatory in PA proceedings. Conducted by *psicólogo*, *assistente social*, or *equipe interprofissional* attached to the *Vara da Família*.
- **Conselho Federal de Psicologia (CFP)** has issued *Notas Técnicas* and *Resoluções* on the methodological constraints applicable to psychological evaluation in PA cases; the CFP position has been more sceptical of PAS than the legislative position.
- **Equipe interprofissional** under ECA arts. 150 ff. — standing multidisciplinary support to the *Vara da Família* / *Vara da Infância*.
- **Depoimento especial / escuta especializada** under Lei 13.431/2017 — protective child-hearing procedures.

## Reunification programs

- **Art. 6º Lei 12.318/2010** sets a graduated catalogue:
  - I — *advertência* (warning)
  - II — *ampliação do regime de convivência familiar*
  - III — *multa*
  - IV — *acompanhamento psicológico ou biopsicossocial*
  - V — *alteração da guarda*
  - VI — *fixação cautelar do domicílio da criança*
  - VII — *suspensão da autoridade parental*
- Conjur and STJ commentary (2024–2026) emphasise that ordering must follow the catalogue's logic and that V–VII require strong evidentiary support.
- US/Anglo-style coercive residential reunification camps are not part of the Brazilian toolkit.

## Domestic-violence interaction

- **Lei Maria da Penha (11.340/2006)** — *medidas protetivas de urgência* are issued by the *Juizado de Violência Doméstica*; the interaction with family-court *guarda* decisions is the central point of contention in the PL 1.372/2023 reform debate.
- The *Conselho Nacional de Direitos Humanos*, the *Conselho Nacional de Saúde*, and the UN Special Rapporteur on Violence against Women and Girls have all recommended revocation of Lei 12.318/2010 on the ground that the law has been used to discount intra-family-violence and sexual-abuse allegations.
- The STJ Terceira Turma's *no-automaticity* doctrine (REsp 2.108.750/GO and adjacent rulings) is the principal current judicial counterweight while the legislative debate continues.

## Honest gaps

- Exact *data de julgamento* and DJe publication date for REsp 1.859.228/SP and REsp 2.108.750/GO are **not publicly confirmed** in the open-web sources surveyed and should be verified at scon.stj.jus.br.
- The interaction between Lei Maria da Penha *medidas protetivas* and family-court *guarda compartilhada* default under Lei 13.058/2014 is not consistently resolved across state tribunals.
- The fate of PL 1.372/2023 in the Senado Federal and any subsequent STF litigation is unresolved as of this reference date.
