# AntiAlienate — Open Knowledge Base

A curated, open-source knowledge base on parental alienation: legal references by jurisdiction, foundational frameworks, value-card infographics, blog content, and credited upstream publisher catalogues.

License: **CC BY 4.0** unless otherwise noted on individual files. Attribution: [AntiAlienate.com](https://www.antialienate.com).

## Sections

- **[/jurisdictions/](./jurisdictions/)** — Per-country and per-US-state legal-reference pages (statutes, custody factors, enforcement mechanisms, case law). 30+ jurisdictions and growing.
- **[/landmark-cases/](./landmark-cases/)** — The cases practitioners and self-represented parents should be able to cite from memory: Troxel v. Granville, Re C [2023], Strand Lobben v Norway, and more.
- **[/research/](./research/)** — Scholarly and research resources: Bernet 5-Factor, Warshak 50/50 consensus, brain-development literature, ABA bias paper, ICD-11 QE52.2.
- **[/open-source/infographics/](./open-source/infographics/)** — Purpose-built shareable knowledge cards: vocabulary, statistics, frameworks, tactics. Designed to be embedded, reposted, and used in advocacy/educational contexts.
- **[/publishers/](./publishers/)** — Independent publishers we mirror, draw from, or credit. Verified Upstream Publishers index.
- **[/blog-posts/](./blog-posts/)** *(if present)* — Long-form blog content mirrored from antialienate.com.
- **[/infographics/heros/](./infographics/heros/)** *(legacy)* — Facebook-timeline hero images. Will be deprecated in favour of `/open-source/infographics/` value cards.
