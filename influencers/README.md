# Influencers — Major Figures in the Parental-Alienation Field

The researchers, clinicians, and practitioners whose published work forms the empirical and theoretical base of the field. Each page links to their work, explains where their contribution sits, and points to specific value cards / templates / case-law that reference them.

- **[Dr. William Bernet, MD](./william-bernet.md)** — 5-Factor Model, Eight Manifestations, ICD-11 QE52.2 advocacy. The diagnostic spine.
- **[Dr. Richard Warshak, PhD](./richard-warshak.md)** — Family Bridges / Welcoming Our Children Home. Leading reunification protocol.
- **[Dr. Amy J.L. Baker, PhD](./amy-baker.md)** — Adult children of alienation. Foundational qualitative research.
- **[Dr. Jennifer Harman, PhD](./jennifer-harman.md)** — Population-level prevalence research. Sociological framing.
- **[Dr. Edward Kruk, PhD](./edward-kruk.md)** — Equal-parenting presumption, ICSP founding.
- **[Dr. Demosthenes Lorandos, PhD, JD](./demosthenes-lorandos.md)** — Forensic methodology, Daubert admissibility.
