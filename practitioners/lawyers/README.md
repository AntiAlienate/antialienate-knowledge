## Structural finding (LATAM batch)

Outside Brazil, LATAM apex-court jurisprudence has consolidated **against** PA recognition 2017-2025:
- **Mexico** — SCJN AI 11/2016 (Oaxaca) + AI 74/2024 (Tlaxcala) struck down vague PA definitions
- **Argentina** — CSJN P.B. v B.K.E. (2021) recognises but limits
- **Colombia** — Corte Constitucional T-526/23 proscribes SAP outright
- **Chile** — 2023 SAP-as-violence bill rejected by Congress

The Brazilian IBDFAM recognition camp (Madaleno / Pereira / Rosa) is now the regional outlier — contested at home by the AAIG/Themis STF ADI 6273 challenge + Projeto de Lei 1.372/2023.
