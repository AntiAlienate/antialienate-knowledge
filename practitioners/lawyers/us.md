# US Lawyers — Parental Alienation Practitioner Directory

**Schema version:** 1.0
**Last updated:** 2026-05-26
**License:** CC BY 4.0 (intended for github.com/AntiAlienate/antialienate-knowledge)
**Scope:** US-admitted attorneys who have (a) handled PA cases at appellate level, (b) published in the field, or (c) testified/consulted as expert witnesses. Stance is sourced, not inferred.

**Standards:** All entries verified from primary sources (firm sites, bar profiles, published works, court records, named press coverage). Where a fact could not be confirmed it is flagged "**not publicly confirmed**".

**Stance taxonomy:**
- `recognition` — treats PA as a legitimate, diagnosable phenomenon worth litigating
- `critique` — argues PA is misused in family court, especially against DV survivors
- `middle` — engages both family violence and alienating-behaviour frames; AFCC-aligned differentiated approach
